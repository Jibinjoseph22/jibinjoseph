About Me

I'm Jibin Joseph, a passionate final-year Computer Science student at Yenepoya Institute of Technology, Mangalore. Hailing from Kannur, Kerala, I have a deep interest in cybersecurity and full-stack development, with a focus on ethical hacking and secure software design.

My journey into cybersecurity began with a strong passion for ethical hacking, leading me to earn my Certified Penetration Tester certification from RedTeam Hacker Academy, Kochi. This certification has equipped me with advanced skills in identifying and mitigating security vulnerabilities, preparing me to address real-world cyber threats effectively.

Beyond cybersecurity, I am also proficient in full-stack web development, particularly with the Django framework, where I specialize in creating robust, secure, and scalable applications. My most recent project, a Medical Integration and Data Information System, reflects my commitment to blending innovation with secure data management.

With a unique combination of skills in penetration testing, cybersecurity, and web development, I am dedicated to leveraging my knowledge to make the digital world a safer place.
